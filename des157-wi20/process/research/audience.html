Audience

Analyzing the target audience

By Matthew Kwong | February 18, 2020

Since doing my academic research on the problem of cyberbullying, I've come up with adjustments to my project. Due to the high amount of students in schools being bullied, I plan on developing an application that would allow students to communicate on a forum about bullying and have access to report bullies to school officials anonymously by screenshotting the content to teachers.

This would differ from other applications out there becuase it would be a free application to use instead of a parental monitoring subscription service.

While analying the potential user demographic, I would definitely say that this application is geared towards students.

  • Student:
    • Age 10-18
    • School
    • Cyberbullied
    • Likes:
      • Instagram
      • Snapchat

SWOT Analysis

Strengths:

  • Allows cyberbully victims to report bullies to school officials anonymously, so they are less likely to have to face the bully
  • Free service
  • Protection
  • Community of other victims of bullying

Weaknesses:

  • Guranteed solution to getting help
  • Student continues to get bullied and cannot find resources

Opportunities:

  • Stop bullying from happening
  • Create a network and safe space for studnets to reach out and ask for advice and speak up about bullies.

Threats:

  • People bully on the forum
