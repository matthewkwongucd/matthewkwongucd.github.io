Sketches

When first opening the page, students will be introduced to sign up and to report bullying.

To think about how to create a conceptual model for students to interact with, I decided to create 2 core different pages. The first being the Forum.

Here students would be able to find resources and ask for help on how to approach and solve their bullying problems from peers. Next, there will be a report feature where students can report bullies to their teachers anonymously with proof of bullying. Additionally, students will be encouraged to reach out for help to talk directly to their advisors.
